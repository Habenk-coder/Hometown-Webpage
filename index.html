Visit Manchester

Manchester is the birthplace of the industrial revolution, the innovator of the first computer and the first passenger railway line and the home of English football.

Top three sites you have to visit in Manchester

I recommend you to see John Rylands library

The John Rylands Research Institute and Library is the most remarkable university special collections library in the world.

You should go to Manchester-Art-Gallery

It is a fine art gallery that houses the city's world-class art collections. which includes more than 2000 oil paintings and 3000 watercolours and drawings. It has a collection of more than 25,000 objects.

Go and watch football

In England football has alwyas been more than just a sport, it's a reflection of life itself.On the field teamwork,discipline, and preseverance are essential in everyday life.
